Project Description

This RC hovercraft is a fully 3D-printed, modular vehicle designed for land and water traversal. It features two brushless DC motors—one providing vertical lift and the other driving forward thrust—along with a servo-controlled rudder for precise steering. The lift system channels air into a simple but effective skirt made from a household trash bag, allowing the craft to float on a cushion of air. All components are secured using M3 hardware and heat-set inserts, making the design robust, easily serviceable, and ideal for DIY experimentation.

Project Details

  • Dual BLDC motor system with separate lift and thrust control.
  • Fully 3D-printed frame using M3 hardware and heat-set inserts.
  • Servo-controlled rudder for precise directional movement.
  • Lightweight air skirt constructed from a standard trash bag.
